Why Sachin?

I'm a UX Designer at Tata Consultancy Services, where I've spent 2.5+ years redesigning enterprise-scale products in the health insurance domain for Cigna Healthcare — a platform serving 100,000+ users.

What makes me different? I'm not just a pixel pusher. I think in systems, move in sprints, and speak the language of both users and engineers. My Figma files are developer-ready. My prototypes are stakeholder-ready. My metrics are business-ready.

I built a design system from scratch (Lumina), redesigned Razorpay's checkout failure states to reduce drop-off and double-charge anxiety, and designed a gym app that finally makes sense for Indian users (RepTrack).

Right now, I'm focused on solving problems in consumer fintech and enterprise UX — whether it's designing credit experiences for India's next generation of users or building scalable systems that improve productivity at enterprise scale.
